# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection and Storage

Bookie Beats Companion respects your privacy and operates with minimal data collection:

### What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ **No personal information** (names, emails, addresses)
- ❌ **No browsing history** outside of supported sites
- ❌ **No financial data** (account balances, transaction history)
- ❌ **No tracking** across websites
- ❌ **No analytics** or telemetry sent to external servers

### What We DO Store Locally

- ✅ **Extension preferences** (odds display format, chart visibility)
- ✅ **Site-specific settings** stored only in your browser's local storage

### Data Handling

- All settings are stored **locally in your browser only**
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- Extension works **entirely offline** after installation

### Permissions Explained

The extension requests these browser permissions:

| Permission  | Purpose                      | Data Access                |
| ----------- | ---------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| `activeTab` | Detect which site you're on  | Current tab URL only       |
| `storage`   | Save your preferences        | Local browser storage only |
| `scripting` | Apply interface improvements | No data collection         |

### Third-Party Sites

This extension operates on third-party prediction market websites (currently Kalshi.com). We:

- Do not modify or intercept any financial transactions
- Do not access account information or trading data
- Only enhance the visual interface of these sites
